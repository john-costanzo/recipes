* Chocolate Cream Pudding Pie
** Ingredients:
- 1 cup sugar
- ⅓ cup cornstarch
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 cups milk
- 4 egg yolks, slightly beaten
- 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 ounces unsweetened chocolate, chopped
** Method:
1. Bake pie shell and let cool.
2. Mix sugar, cornstarch and salt in 1½ quart saucepan.
3. Stir in milk gradually.
4. Add chocolate.
5. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils.
6. Boil and stir 1 minute.
7. Stir at least half of the hot mixture gradually into egg yolks.
8. Blend into hot mixture in saucepan.
9. Boil and stir 1 minute.
10. Remove from heat.
11. Stir in vanilla.
12. Pour into pie shell.
13. Press plastic wrap onto filling.
14. Refrigerate at least 2 hours but no longer than 48 hours.
15. Remove plastic wrap.
16. Top pie with whipped cream, if desired.
