* Aunt Anna's Apple Cake

** Ingredients:
- 4 cups cut up apples (sprinkle with sugar, let stand)
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten
- ¾ cup oil
- 1½ teaspoon baking soda
- 2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- nuts and/or raisins

** Method:
1. Mix by hand. Grease pan.
2. Bake 350 degrees for 50 minutes.
