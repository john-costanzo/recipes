* Vanilla Cream Pie
** Ingredients:
- ⅔ cup sugar
- ¼ cup cornstarch
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 cups milk
- 4 egg yolks, slightly beaten
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 ounces unsweetened chocolate, chopped
** Method:
1. Bake pie shell and let cool.
2. Mix sugar, cornstarch and salt in 1½ quart saucepan.
3. Stir in milk gradually.
4. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils.
5. Boil and stir 1 minute.
6. Stir at least half of the hot mixture gradually into egg yolks.
7. Blend into hot mixture in saucepan.
8. Boil and stir 1 minute.
9. Remove from heat.
10. Stir in butter and vanilla.
11. Pour into pie shell.
12. Press plastic wrap onto filling.
13. Refrigerate at least 2 hours but no longer than 48 hours.
14. Remove plastic wrap.
15. Top pie with whipped cream, if desired.
