* Banana Crumb Muffins
** Ingredients:
*** Topping
- ½ cup (60g) King Arthur unbleached all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup (50g) sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 4 tablespoons (½ stick) butter or margarine, room temperature
*** Muffins
- 1½ (180g) cups King Arthur unbleached all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 large, ripe bananas, mashed
- ¾ (150g) cup sugar
- 1 egg, slightly beaten (or 1 tablespoon flaxseed + 3 tablespoons water sitting for ten minutes)
- ⅓ cup butter or margarine, melted
- ¾ cup chopped walnut (/optional/)
** Method:
*** For the topping:
1. In a medium bowl, mix flour, sugar and cinnamon.
2. Add butter or margarine and mix with a fork or pastry cutter until crumbly.
3. Put aside while preparing muffin batter.
*** For the muffins:
1. In large bowl, combine dry ingredients and set aside.
2. In another bowl, combine mashed bananas, sugar, slightly beaten egg and melted butter or margarine.
3. Mix well.
4. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.
5. Fill greased muffin cups two-thirds full. (Do not use paper muffin cups!)
6. Using hands, arrange coarse, pea-sized crumbs over muffin batter.
7. Bake at 375° for 18 to 20 minutes or until muffins test done with a cake tester.
8. Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack.
