* Breakfast Casserole
** Ingredients:
*** For the potatoes
- 3 Yukon Gold potatoes, about 1 pound, chopped into ¼-inch pieces, or 1 pound frozen hash brown potatoes, see notes*
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
*** For the casserole
- 12 large eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1¼ teaspoons sea salt
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for the baking dish
- ½ medium yellow onion, chopped
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, stemmed, seeded, and chopped
- 1 green bell pepper, stemmed, seeded, and chopped
- 1 cup fresh spinach, roughly chopped
- 4 green onions, chopped
- 1½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
** Method:
*** Roast the potatoes
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Place the potatoes on the baking sheet.
3. Drizzle with the olive oil and sprinkle with the salt and several grinds of pepper.
4. Toss to coat.
5. Spread evenly on the baking sheet and roast for 20 to 25 minutes, or until tender and browned.
*** Make the casserole
1. Reduce the oven temperature to 350°F and oil a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and ¼ teaspoon of the salt.
3. Set aside.
4. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Add the onion, the remaining 1 teaspoon salt, and several grinds of pepper.
6. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 to 8 minutes, or until soft.
7. Add the garlic, red pepper, and green pepper and cook, stirring occasionally, for 2 minutes.
8. Add the spinach and half the green onions and toss until the spinach is just wilted.
9. Spread the potatoes in an even layer in the prepared baking dish.
10. Top with 1 cup of the cheese, followed by the sautéed veggies.
11. Pour in the egg mixture and sprinkle with the remaining ½ cup cheese and the reserved green onions.
12. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes, or until the eggs are set.
13. Let stand for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
#+begin_note
   Using frozen hash browns: To simplify this recipe, you can use 1 pound diced frozen hash brown potatoes instead of the roasted potatoes. Let thaw before adding to the casserole. When I use hash browns, I typically need to bake the casserole a few minutes longer for it to set.
