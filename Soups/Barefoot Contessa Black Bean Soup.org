* Barefoot Contessa Black Bean Soup
** Ingredients:
- 4 cups, cooked (or 2 (15-ounce) cans, rinsed and drained) Black beans
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large yellow onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, peeled and diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 4 cups vegetable stock
- 2 Tbsp tomato paste
- 1 bay leaf
- salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
- Optional toppings:
    + sour cream
    + diced avocado
    + fresh cilantro
    + shredded cheese
    + tortilla chips
** Method:
*** Sauté the Aromatics
1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add the diced onion, garlic, carrots, celery, and red bell pepper.
3. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are softened and fragrant.
*** Add Spices and Tomato Paste
1. Stir in cumin, chili powder, paprika, and tomato paste.
2. Cook for 1-2 minutes to toast the spices and deepen the flavors.
*** Add Beans and Stock
1. Add the black beans, chicken or vegetable stock, and bay leaf.
2. Stir to combine.
3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer uncovered for 25-30 minutes.
*** Blend for Creaminess
1. Remove the bay leaf.
2. Use an immersion blender to partially blend the soup, leaving some beans and vegetables intact for texture.
3. Alternatively, blend half the soup in a blender and return it to the pot.
*** Season and Finish
1. Stir in the lime juice and season with salt and pepper to taste.
2. Simmer for another 5 minutes to meld the flavors.
*** Serve and Garnish
1. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with your favorite toppings, such as sour cream, avocado, cilantro, or tortilla chips.
