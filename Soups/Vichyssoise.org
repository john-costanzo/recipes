* Vichyssoise
** Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
- 1½ cups sliced leeks
- 3 cups vegetable broth
- 2 cups peeled potatoes, cut into 1-inch cubes
- ½ cup half-and-half
- ⅛ teaspoon ground (white) pepper
- snipped fresh chives for garnish
** Method:
1. In a 2-quart saucepan, melt the butter or margarine over medium-high heat.
2. Add leeks; cook, stirring, until softened, about 1 minute.
3. Add the broth and bring to a boil.
4. Add the potatoes; reduce heat and simmer, uncovered 25 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
5. Place the soup in a blender or food processor container fitted with a steel blade.
6. Cover and process until smooth.
7. Add half-and-half, and pepper.
8. Chill
9. Served garnished with snipped fresh chives.
