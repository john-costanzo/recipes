* Chickpeas in Instant Pot
** Ingredients:
- 1 cup dried chickpeas (about 200–225 g), picked over and rinsed
- Water (see amounts below)
- 1 to 1½ teaspoons salt (/optional but recommended/)
** Method:
1. Soak 1 cup dried chickpeas in at least 2–3 cups water for 8–12 hours, then drain and rinse.
2. Add soaked chickpeas to the Instant Pot with about 3–4 cups fresh water, covering by about 1 inch; add 1 teaspoon salt if using.
3. Close lid, set valve to Sealing, select Pressure Cook on High and set:
     - 15–23 minutes for tender, intact chickpeas (salads, stews).
     - 25–30 minutes for softer, creamier chickpeas.
4. ​  Let pressure release naturally 15–20 minutes, then vent any remaining steam and open.
5. ​  Check doneness and add a few more minutes on High if you want them softer.
     #+begin_tip
     For extra flavor, add a halved onion, a few garlic cloves, bay leaf, or herbs to the pot; discard aromatics after cooking.
     #+end_tip
     #+begin_note
     If not pre-soaked:
     - 40–45 minutes for tender chickpeas with slight bite (good for salads, curries).
     - 55–60 minutes for very soft chickpeas (good for hummus).
     #+end_note
** Yield
- Cooking 1 cup of dried chickpeas (roughly 200–225 g) in the Instant Pot yields about 440–500 g of cooked chickpeas, drained, which is around 2.5 cups by volume.
