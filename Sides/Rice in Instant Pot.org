* Rice in Instant Pot
** Ingredients:
- 1 cup long-grain brown rice (rinsed well until water runs clear)
- 1¼ cups water (or low-sodium broth for flavor)
- ¼ tsp salt (/optional/)
- ½ tsp oil or butter (prevents sticking) (/optional/)
** Method:
1. Rinse rice in a fine-mesh strainer under cold water for 30–60 seconds to remove excess starch.
2. Add rinsed rice, water, salt, and oil/butter (if using) to the inner pot; stir briefly.
3. ​Secure lid, set valve to Sealing, select Pressure Cook (High) for 22 minutes.
4. ​Allow natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure.
5. ​Fluff with a fork, rest 5 minutes covered, then serve.
