* Greek Vinaigrette
** Ingredients:
- ½ cup olive oil
- ½ cup red wine vinegar
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp dry oregano
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp sugar
** Method:
1. Mix all ingredients and serve.
