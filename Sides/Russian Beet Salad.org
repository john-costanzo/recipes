* Russian Beet Salad
** Ingredients:
- 1 beet
- 2 potatoes
- 2 carrots (optional)
- 1 onion (medium)
- 4 pickles (small)
- olive oil
- vinegar or lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
** Method:
1. Cube all veggies.
2. Boil veggies until tender, then cool them.
3. Add oil, vinegar or lemon juice and salt and pepper.
4. Mix together.
5. Chill and serve.
   #+begin_tip
   Optional: add sauerkraut and/or canned red kidney beans.
   #+end_tip
