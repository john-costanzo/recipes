* Black Beans in Instant Pot
** Ingredients:
- ⅔ cup dried black beans (soaked 8-12 hours, then drained and rinsed)
- 3 cups water
*** Optional flavorings: 
    + ¼ onion
    + ½ teaspoon salt
    + ½ teaspoon black pepper
    + 1 bay leaf
    + 2 garlic cloves
    + ½ teaspoon cumin
    + ½ teaspoon oregano
    + ½ teaspoon chili powder or red pepper flakes
** Method:
1. Cook:
     a. For firmer beans (good for salads): cook on High Pressure for 8–9 minutes.
     b. For softer, creamier beans: cook on High Pressure for 10–11 minutes.
2. Natural release 15 minutes before venting.
** Yield
- About 28 ounces of cooked beans.
