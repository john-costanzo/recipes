* Black Beans in Instant Pot
** Ingredients:
- ⅔ cup dried black beans (about 5 ounces) (soaked 8-12 hours, then drained and rinsed)
- 1½ - 2 cups water (enough to cover beans by 1-2 inches)
*** Optional flavorings: 
    + ¼ onion
    + ½ teaspoon salt
    + ½ teaspoon black pepper
    + 1 bay leaf
    + 2 garlic cloves
    + ½ teaspoon cumin
    + ½ teaspoon oregano
    + ½ teaspoon chili powder or red pepper flakes
** Method:
*** Soak:
     a. Cover beans with water and soak overnight (8-12 hours).
     b. Drain.
*** Cook:
     a. For firmer beans (good for salads): cook on high pressure for 8–9 minutes.
     b. For softer, creamier beans (good for burgers or refritos): cook on high pressure for 10–11 minutes.
     c. Allow natural pressure release for 10-15 minutes before venting.
** Yield
- Just shy of 15 ounces of cooked beans  (drained weight after cooking, equivalent to one standard can).
